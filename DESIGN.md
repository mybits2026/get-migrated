# Design System - Get Migrated (PR Readiness MVP)

## Product Context
- **What this is:** A lean, V1 PR Readiness Concierge landing page. Unbundling the migration process by providing clarity and preparation before handing off to licensed experts.
- **Who it's for:** Nepali students and graduates already in Australia planning their PR pathway.
- **Project type:** Lead-generation and intake concierge.

## Aesthetic Direction
- **Direction:** Editorial trust with high clarity.
- **Mood:** Serious, calm, informed, "no-bs". The page should feel like a useful utility.

## Typography
- **Display/Hero:** `Fraunces` - Adds gravity and distinction.
- **Body & UI:** `Instrument Sans` - Clean, contemporary, and readable.
- **Data/Tables/Code:** `IBM Plex Mono` - Gives process details a factual, system-like tone.

## Color
- **Primary:** `#0F2747` (Trust/Navy) - Core trust color for headings and major surfaces.
- **Secondary:** `#2F7D6D` (Eucalyptus) - Used for route signals and metadata.
- **Accent:** `#FF7A59` (Coral/Action) - Reserved for primary actions and CTAs.
- **Neutrals:** `#F8FAFC` (Cloud) background, `#F4E7D3` (Sand) warm support surface, `#1B1B1B` (Ink) body text.

## Spacing & Layout
- **Base unit:** `8px`
- **Approach:** Minimalist, single-column primary flow.
- **Max content width:** `1280px`
- **Border radius:** `sm: 16px`, `md: 24px`, `lg: 32px`, `full: 9999px`
